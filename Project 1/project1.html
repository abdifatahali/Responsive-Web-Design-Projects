Here is Some Info of Mr.Paul Walker :

  • 1973 : Paul Walker was born in Glendale, California.
  • 1985 : He starred his small screen career in a television commercial.
  • 1986 : Walker's film career began.
  • 2001 : Walker's breakthrough role arrived from action film The Fast and the Furious.
  • 2002 : MTV Movie Award - Best On-Screen Team (shared with Vin Diesel)
  • 2003 : Walker as a notable film star and leading man in sequel 2 Fast 2 Furious.
  • 2006 : His movie at the box office, grossing over US$20 million during its first weekend.
  • 2008 : Walker starred in the independent film The Lazarus Project.
  • 2011 : Teen Choice Award - shared with Vin Diesel and Dwayne Johnson.
  • 2013 : Pawn Shop Chronicles, which was Walker's last film released before his death.
  • 2015 : The song "See You Again", was a tribute to Walker.
  • 2015 : Teen Choice Award - for Furious 7.
  • 2013 : Walker died - as a passenger in a single-car crash alongside friend and driver Roger Rodas.
